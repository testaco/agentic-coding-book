**External Resources**:

**On Requirements and Specifications**:

- Mavin, Alistair and Wilkinson, Philip. "EARS (Easy Approach to Requirements Syntax)" - The original EARS notation paper
- Wiegers, Karl and Beatty, Joy. "Software Requirements, 3rd Edition" - Comprehensive requirements engineering
- OpenAPI Specification: <https://swagger.io/specification/> - REST API specification standard
- AsyncAPI Specification: <https://www.asyncapi.com/> - Event-driven API specification

**On Testing and Quality**:

- Fowler, Martin. "Testing Strategies in a Microservice Architecture" - <https://martinfowler.com/articles/microservice-testing/>
- "The Practical Test Pyramid" by Ham Vocke - <https://martinfowler.com/articles/practical-test-pyramid.html>
- Marick, Brian. "Testing Foundations" - <http://www.exampler.com/testing-com/>

**On Architecture and Design**:

- Vernon, Vaughn. "Domain-Driven Design Distilled" - Bounded contexts and clear boundaries
- Ford, Neal et al. "Building Evolutionary Architectures" - Designing for change
- Martin, Robert C. "Clean Architecture" - Interface design and dependency management

**On Product Management and Decision-Making**:

- Cagan, Marty. "Inspired: How to Create Tech Products Customers Love" - Product vision and decision-making
- Ries, Eric. "The Lean Startup" - MVP thinking and rapid iteration
- Olsen, Dan. "The Lean Product Playbook" - Outcome-based product development

**On Velocity and Bottlenecks**:

- Goldratt, Eliyahu M. "The Goal" - Theory of Constraints (identifying bottlenecks in systems)
- Kim, Gene et al. "The DevOps Handbook" - Flow, feedback, and continuous improvement
- Forsgren, Nicole et al. "Accelerate" - Measuring and improving software delivery performance
